Stecher Don- Exton Xfinity Store

Visit location: Exton Xfinity Store - 106 Bartlett Ave, Exton, PA 19341
Date: 11/19/16
Time: 11:00 am
Overall impression: Very clean store, customer representatives were very helpful to customers in the store. Products presentations were easily accessible and provided a much better experience than the prior Xfinity store.
Suggestions for better customer experience:
  1. Update the new location brochure with pictures of store front and text that states they are next to babies R us. Send out another mailer to help the customers find the new store.
    • Add location of store to X1 STB and Comcast apps
  2. Community outreach – find ways to get the store involved in local community events, to spread the word of customer focus and new products and offerings.
  3. Focus on content as well as X1, and Xfinity Home. In terms of content, to be specific, store can advertise (promote) availability of high quality viewing experience of upcoming Rio summer Olympics on Comcast network.
  4. Customer focus was main motto of the store manager and it was visible from his actions.
  5. Kiosk labels or customer representative pictures for the kiosks so the customer knows which kiosk to visit when it’s their time to speak with a representative.
  6. Mis-representation of data to collect customer footfall in the store due to “greeter” at the door. Ways should be find to offset that additional figures.
  7. Customer Queue Management software had some issues due to which it keep on showing customers in waiting. It may also be induced due to the facts that process of check-in was not properly followed by Customer executives.
  8. Moreover, customer Queue manager software prompt Counter #, but there were no counter number in store, which was confusing, if more people are waiting.
  9. New devices launched by Comcast should be displayed and different ones should be used in each TV to show variety
  10. People were still getting guidance for automatic payment kiosk. Can we have a video playing on a screen near kiosk?
  11. Add a coffee machine to the lobby.
  12. Price is also a crucial element in making a purchase decision. All cable providers are aggressively promoting their package by highlighting their package prices. Can the store also have promotion materials (video, banners, pamphlet, etc) highlighting price advantage for new and existing customers?

Stecher Don- Tier 3 Customer support

Visit location: Tier 3 customer support team Date: 7/20/16 Time: 11:00 am Overall impression: The representatives were very knowledgeable about our products and services. They were quick to resolve customer problems. Suggestions for better customer experience:
  1. They discussed the many ticketing systems they interact with and the different types of tickets available to them. For example, when a customer calls there are 5 types of tickets that can be generated.
    • 5 basic TTS tickets: SR - Service request, CR – Customer reported, CM – Change Management, OE – Outage Event, SI – Service Impacting
  2. Tickets escalated up to their level, may reference a TTS ticket but most of the time they need to enter more tickets in other systems, or send e-mails to fix the customers problems.
  3. They have to utilize many tools to resolve customer problems. Since we only had limited time, I reviewed the following tools: Einstein 360, Watchtower, Xray.
    1. Einstein 360 – is the most widely used tool, with Legacy, X1 and customer billing info
    2. Xray – is specific to X1 devices, with debugging information, billing info, code versions
    3. WatchTower – Is overlaid over google maps and can show outages, RF problems, report connected device
Some of the concerns the tier 3 folks shared, was the non connected ticketing systems. The number of tools they use and how rapidly the tools change along with new tools, however they didn’t think their was much they could do about the tooling changes. After visiting with these team members, I believe it would take a lot more time and in-depth reviewing of their systems, tools and processes to help improve their situation.
